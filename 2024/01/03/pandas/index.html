pd.DataFrame and pd.read_csv

This blog is about the key use and attributes of a famous data structure from pandas, pd.DataFrame, including fetch a row, column and element, as well as the transformation of List and DataFrame.

一. 读取数据pd.read_csv(filepath)

1. Args 说明

  1. filePath = 'path/to/your/example.csv'

  2. default将第一行读取为列头，如果第一列为数据，则指定 header=None,此时会添加默认列头(0,1,2,3,…)

2. 读取excel文件

  1. 使用如下代码将excel转化成csv f1=[99.06,99.15,97.77,98.40,99.25]
    precision=[98.97,99.18,96.67,99.80,99.52]
    accuracy=[99.06,99.15,97.73,98.41,99.27]
    recall=[99.16,99.12,99.98,97.03,98.99]

import pandas as pd
result=pd.DataFrame({‘f1’:f1,’recall’:recall,’precision’:precision,’accuracy’:accuracy}).applymap(“{:.04f}”.format)
result.loc[len(result)]=[sum(recall)/5,sum(f1)/5,sum(accuracy)/5,sum(precision)/5]
result.rename(index={5:’average’},inplace=True)
result
